Last Updated: November 5, 2014

This Privacy Policy explains how information about you is collected, used and disclosed by Shoppinghook Pty Ltd. and our subsidiaries (collectively, "Charge Cuz") when you use our websites, mobile applications and other online products and services (collectively, the "Services") or when you otherwise interact with us.

We may change this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will notify you by revising the date at the top of the policy and, in some cases, we may provide you with additional notice (such as adding a statement to our websites' homepages or sending you a notification). We encourage you to review the Privacy Policy whenever you access the Services to stay informed about our information practices and the ways you can help protect your privacy.

Collection of Information

Information You Provide to Us

We collect information you provide directly to us. For example, we collect information when you create an account, use the Services to send or receive a messages, request customer support or otherwise communicate with us. The types of information we may collect include your username, password, email address, age, gender and any other information you choose to provide.

When you send or receive messages, we also process and store the contents and details of the messages on our servers. The contents of messages are also temporarily stored on the devices of recipients. We also cannot prevent others from making copies of your messages or photos (e.g., by taking a screenshot). In addition, as for any other digital information, there may be ways to access messages while still in temporary storage on recipients' devices or, forensically, even after they are deleted. You should not use Charge Cuz to send messages if you want to be certain that the recipient cannot keep a copy.

Information We Collect Automatically When You Use the Services

When you access or use our Services, we automatically collect information about you, including:

  • Usage Information: When you send or receive messages, we collect information about these messages, including the time, date, sender and recipient, theme and what audio was sent, what audio the receiver responded too. We also collect information about the number of Charge Cuz's sent and received between you and your friends and which friends you exchange Charge Cuz's with most frequently.
  • Log Information: We log information about your use of our websites, including your browser type and language, access times, pages viewed, your IP address and the website you visited before navigating to our websites.
  • Device Information: We may collect information about the computer or device you use to access our Services, including the hardware model, operating system and version, MAC address, unique device identifier, phone number, International Mobile Equipment Identity ("IMEI") and mobile network information. In addition, the Services may access your device's native phonebook and image storage applications, with your consent, to facilitate your use of certain features of the Services. For more information about how you can control our access to these applications, please see "Your Choices" below.
  • Location Information: With your consent, we may collect information about the location of your device to facilitate your use of certain features of our Services, determine the speed at which your device is traveling, add your local weather conditions to your Charge Cuz's, and for any other purpose described in this privacy policy. For more information about how you can control our collection of location information, please see "Your Choices" below.
  • Information Collected by Cookies and Other Tracking Technologies: We use various technologies to collect information, and this may include sending cookies to you. Cookies are small data files stored on your hard drive or in device memory that help us to improve our Services and your experience, see which areas and features of our Services are popular and count visits. We may also collect information using web beacons (also known as "tracking pixels").

Information We Collect From Other Sources

We may also obtain information from other sources and combine that with information we collect through our Services. For instance, when you complete in-app purchases from your mobile device, we may collect information about you from the provider of your device operating system when they process the purchases on our behalf. Your Choices

Privacy Settings

Through settings in our Services, you can choose whether to accept messages only from users you have added to your friends list, or from any other Charge Cuz user who determines your name. If you choose to receive messages only from your friends, and a user who is not on your friends list attempts to send you a message or add you as a friend, you will receive a notification and have the option of adding the user to your friends list.

You also have the ability to delete users from your friends list or to block specific users at any time by using controls within our Services.

Device Information

Certain features of the Services may require access to your device's native phonebook and image storage applications (e.g., to find friends or to store a profile picture in your photo gallery). If you choose to find friends, the names and phone numbers in your device's native phonebook will be transmitted to Charge Cuz. If you later decide to revoke your consent and prevent us from continuing to access your phonebook and image storage applications, you can do so following the standard uninstall process and removing our Services from your device. On iOS devices, you can grant or revoke your consent at any time and prevent us from continuing to access your phonebook and image storage applications by changing the settings on your device.

Location

When you first launch any features of our Services that collect location information, you will be asked to consent to our collection of this information. You may also stop our collection of location information by following the standard uninstall process to remove our Services from your device.

Cookies

Most web browsers are set to accept cookies by default. If you prefer, you can usually choose to set your browser to remove or reject browser cookies. Please note that if you choose to remove or reject cookies, this could affect the availability and functionality of our website.

Push Notifications/Alerts

Charge Cuz may send push notifications or alerts to your device. You can deactivate these messages at any time by changing the notification settings on your device (iOS) or by changing your settings within our Services (Android) however deactivating this permission will stop Charge Cuz sending notifications to your device.
